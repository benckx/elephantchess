Datasets

Games played by users of the webapp are public and data is available under GPL-3.0 license. The following datasets are anonymized. Non-anonymized game data can be shared with third parties for research or technical purposes ("non-anonymized" meaning showing usernames, but without email address, IP or any personal data).

The ZIP file contains CSV with 1000 games each. When the game was analyzed, engine data is also available in the CSV.

  • Player-vs-Player Xiangqi game moves July 2026 (70.04 MB)
  • Player-vs-Player Xiangqi game moves June 2026 (10.73 MB)



Note: If you need any dataset or data sample from elephantchess (games, puzzles, analysis, player stats, etc.) for any purpose, please contact us rather than scrap. It's always interesting to hear about your projects and it's easier to get you what you might need.
